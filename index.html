Mokotów High School Campus

Our Mokotów High School Campus is a state-of-the-art facility for Years 10-13 (15-19 years), located on the ground floor of the Tulipan House business centre.

The campus is ideally-suited to the learning needs of our senior students, providing first-rate preparation for the IB Diploma Programme (IBDP) and A level examinations that enable their transition to the world’s top universities.

"From our very first day together to this proud moment, we have grown, learned, and created memories that will last a lifetime."

Today, we celebrate not only our achievements, but also the friendships, lessons, and dreams that shaped us.

"As one chapter closes and another begins, we carry the spirit of our school and the strength of our shared journey into the future."
